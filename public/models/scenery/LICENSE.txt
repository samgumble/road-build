Files included in this directory (a small subset of each pack, selected for
tree/house/building silhouettes suitable for stylized development scenery):

nature/ (from Nature Kit, self-contained flat-color materials, no textures)
- tree_default.glb      (round deciduous tree)
- tree_pineRoundC.glb   (rounded pine)
- tree_pineTallA.glb    (tall pine)

suburban/ (from City Kit (Suburban); models reference suburban/Textures/colormap.png)
- building-type-a.glb   (small house)
- building-type-c.glb   (small house, alternate roofline)
- Textures/colormap.png

commercial/ (from City Kit (Commercial); model references commercial/Textures/colormap.png)
- building-skyscraper-e.glb (taller multi-story building)
- Textures/colormap.png

Downloaded verbatim from the official Kenney CDN on 2026-07-02; no
modifications were made to the GLB or texture files themselves (only moved
into per-pack subdirectories alongside their own Textures/colormap.png so each
GLB's relative texture URI still resolves).
